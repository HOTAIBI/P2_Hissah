  • Gestation period: 64 – 67 days
  • average lifespan: 4 – 5 years

The domestic cat (Latin: Felis catus) is a small, typically furry, carnivorous mammal. They are often called house cats when kept as indoor pets. Cats are often valued by humans for companionship and for their ability to hunt vermin. There are more than 70 cat breeds
